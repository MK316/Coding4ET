## 1. Introduction to DataFrames
A DataFrame is a 2D data structure, like a table, with rows and columns. It is part of the pandas library in Python.

For instance,

|ID|Name|Music|Math|
|--|--|--|--|
|1|Mary|87|80|
|2|Tom|67|87|
|3|Jane|90|70|
|...|...|...|...|
|100|Elliot|89|95|
